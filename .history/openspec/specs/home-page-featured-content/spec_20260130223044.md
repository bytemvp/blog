## Purpose

Define featured and recent content presentation on the home page. (TBD)

## Requirements

### Requirement: Featured Content Area
The home page SHALL include a featured content area highlighting recent or curated posts.

#### Scenario: Featured content displayed
- **WHEN** the home page renders
- **THEN** at least one featured post SHALL be displayed with title and excerpt

### Requirement: Latest Posts List
The home page SHALL include a list of recent posts.

#### Scenario: Recent posts shown
- **WHEN** the home page renders
- **THEN** a list of recent posts with titles and dates SHALL be visible
