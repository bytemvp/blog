## Purpose

Define the primary call-to-action behavior for the home page. (TBD)

## Requirements

### Requirement: Primary Call-to-Action
The home page SHALL include a primary call-to-action that directs users to a key destination.

#### Scenario: CTA visible
- **WHEN** the home page loads
- **THEN** a primary CTA button/link SHALL be visible in the hero or CTA section

### Requirement: CTA Destination
The CTA SHALL link to a meaningful destination (e.g., blog index or about page).

#### Scenario: CTA link
- **WHEN** a user clicks the CTA
- **THEN** they SHALL be navigated to the intended destination
