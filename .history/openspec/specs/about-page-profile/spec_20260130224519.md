## Purpose

Define the hero bio and profile summary presentation on the about page. (TBD)

## Requirements

### Requirement: Hero Bio
The about page SHALL include a hero bio section with a clear name/title and short summary.

#### Scenario: Hero bio visible
- **WHEN** the about page loads
- **THEN** a hero bio with name/title and summary SHALL be displayed

### Requirement: Profile Highlights
The about page SHALL present key profile highlights near the hero section.

#### Scenario: Highlights displayed
- **WHEN** the hero section is visible
- **THEN** at least two profile highlights SHALL be shown
