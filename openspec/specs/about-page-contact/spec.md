## Purpose

Define the contact call-to-action for the about page. (TBD)

## Requirements

### Requirement: Contact Section
The about page SHALL include a contact section with a clear call-to-action.

#### Scenario: Contact CTA visible
- **WHEN** the about page loads
- **THEN** a contact CTA button or link SHALL be visible

### Requirement: Contact Destination
The contact CTA SHALL link to a contact method (e.g., email or social profile).

#### Scenario: Contact link
- **WHEN** a user clicks the contact CTA
- **THEN** they SHALL be navigated to the intended destination
