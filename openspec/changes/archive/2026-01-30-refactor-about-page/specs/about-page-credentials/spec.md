## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Timeline Section
The about page SHALL include a timeline section with key milestones.

#### Scenario: Timeline visible
- **WHEN** the about page loads
- **THEN** a timeline list with at least three milestones SHALL be shown

### Requirement: Skills Section
The about page SHALL include a skills section highlighting areas of expertise.

#### Scenario: Skills displayed
- **WHEN** the about page loads
- **THEN** a skills list or grid SHALL be visible
